Our Story

Born from a bold idea

It started in 2016 with two designers and a laptop in a Nairobi co-working space. We were tired of seeing African businesses settle for generic digital products that didn't reflect their ambitions.

So we built NEXUS — a studio that would combine world-class design with emerging technology, and do it with an African perspective that the global market needed.

Today, we're a team of 18 designers, engineers, and strategists working with clients across four continents. Our work has been featured in Fast Company, Wired Africa, and the African Business Design Awards.
